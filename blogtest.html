Marylin

If this scene were a scent it would either be Francis Kurkdjian's OUD Satin Mood (thick with violets, vanilla and of course oud) or Féminité du Bois, once by Shiseido (the original curvy bottle, in all its plum-coloured glory - echoing the curving movements of the two actresses). Both scents are too much, unforgatable but in a good way. Imagine infusing the cinema with one of these olfactory essays or intertexts. Imagine being floored by violet-induced vertigo.
